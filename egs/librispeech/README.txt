
 The LibriSpeech corpus is a large corpus of English read speech derived from
 audiobooks in the LibriVox project.  It was prepared as a speech recognition
 corpus by Vassil Panayotov.

 This corpus will be hosted at www.openslr.org and possibly other places.

 when we finalize everything.
